Rules: exactly one tool is bound to L and one to R at all times. Keyboard shortcuts (P, E, I, T, F, R, L, S, B) always set the L binding. Right-clicking a toolbox tool sets the R binding. The options bar edits whichever binding is selected in its L/R segment; drawing with either button auto-selects that segment.
